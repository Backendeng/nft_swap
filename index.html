FAQ

  • What is Sano Swap?

    A trustless p2p OTC NFT trading protocol focused on security and community.

  • How does it work?

    Please read our How To Trade and check our Discord.

  • Which browser is best to use and can we use on mobile?

    Sano Swap is responsive and works on all browsers but we do recommend Chrome on PC.

  • Which wallet can I use to trade?

    You are able to use Metamask, Coinbase, Wallet Connect, and Fortmatic. New wallets will continue to be added in the future.

  • Should I speed up trx to make the trade?

    We recommend applying the gas amount suggested from the wallet you are trading with and check current gas prices (gwei) at EthGasStation website.

  • What is the Service fee?

    There is a flat fee of 0.01 ETH (or 5 MATIC) when bartering NFT for NFT or a 0.5% fee only to the counterparty when in the situation native tokens are involved in the deal. *Because every trx is onchain there will also need to be paid a gas amount for the approval of NFTs, cancel, and closing of a trade.

  • What are Creator Fees?

    Sano Swap uses the Manifold royalty registry standard which most other NFT markets use and through this system creators of collections can establish their own on-chain royalty %. Meaning creators will earn that revenue % every time their NFT's are sold through any of the registered markets.

    Go to Your Hub & Trades page and there you can find all deals including the pending ones.

  • I am trying to trade an NFT which is in my wallet but I do not see it in the list of collections?

    If you cannot find the collection of NFTs which you are trying to trade then you can manually add it by inputting the Collection Address in the Add NFT Collection box.

  • Can I cancel a trade?

    Yes, any party now can cancel a trade but before the counterparty Trader of the deal closes it.

  • Are ERC20 supported?

    No, currently we support only native tokens. New token announcements will be made once available.
